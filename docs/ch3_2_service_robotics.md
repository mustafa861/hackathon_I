# Chapter 3.2: Service Robotics

Service robotics encompasses humanoid robots designed to assist humans in various service-oriented tasks, from healthcare to hospitality.

## Healthcare Applications

### Medical Assistance
- Surgical robots
- Rehabilitation robots
- Patient monitoring
- Medication dispensing

### Elderly Care
- Companionship robots
- Physical assistance
- Health monitoring
- Emergency response

## Hospitality Services

### Customer Service
- Reception and greeting
- Concierge services
- Information provision
- Guiding and navigation

### Food Service
- Serving robots
- Kitchen assistance
- Order delivery
- Table cleaning

## Educational Applications

### Teaching Assistants
- Interactive learning
- Special education
- Language learning
- STEM education

### Research and Development
- Human-robot interaction studies
- AI behavior research
- Educational technology
- Social robotics research

## Retail and Commercial

### Customer Interaction
- Sales assistance
- Product recommendations
- Inventory management
- Customer analytics

### Security and Surveillance
- Monitoring systems
- Intrusion detection
- Patrol services
- Access control

## Challenges

- Safety and reliability
- Social acceptance
- Cost-effectiveness
- Technical limitations
