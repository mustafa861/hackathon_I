# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook covers the fundamental concepts, advanced techniques, and practical applications of artificial intelligence in physical systems and humanoid robots.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook is divided into three main parts:

1. **Fundamentals of Physical AI** - Understanding the basics of AI in physical systems
2. **Humanoid Robotics** - Design and control of humanoid robots
3. **Advanced Applications** - Real-world applications and future directions

Each part contains three detailed chapters that build upon each other to provide a complete understanding of the subject matter.
