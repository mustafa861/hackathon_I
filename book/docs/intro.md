# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This book explores the cutting-edge intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics, focusing on the development of intelligent, human-like machines.

## About This Book

This book is designed to provide both theoretical foundations and practical insights into the field of humanoid robotics. We will cover topics ranging from the basic principles of AI and robotics to advanced concepts in machine learning, sensor integration, and control systems for humanoid robots.

### What You'll Learn

- Fundamentals of Physical AI and its application in robotics
- Design principles for humanoid robots
- AI algorithms for perception, decision-making, and control
- Integration of sensors and actuators
- Real-world applications and ethical considerations

## Target Audience

This book is intended for:
- Robotics engineers and researchers
- AI/ML practitioners interested in physical systems
- Students studying robotics or AI
- Technology enthusiasts with a background in engineering or computer science

## How to Use This Book

Each chapter builds upon the previous ones, gradually introducing more complex concepts. We encourage you to follow the book sequentially, but you may also jump to specific topics of interest. Interactive elements, such as the RAG chatbot integrated into this book, are available to help answer questions and provide additional context as you learn.

## Getting Started

To get the most out of this book, you should have a basic understanding of:
- Programming concepts (preferably Python)
- Linear algebra and calculus
- Fundamentals of machine learning

We will provide practical examples and code snippets throughout the book to illustrate key concepts.
